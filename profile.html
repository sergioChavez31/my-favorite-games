Super Smash Bros. Ultimate

This is a fighting game that is great for playing on your own or with friends! It has the fighting spirit of Street Fighter and the style of Mario Kart.

Minecraft

This game is great after a long day. You can build whatever you want or survive out in the wild. The choice is yours! (my personal favorite)

Fortnite

This game is great with friends if you're into building and being the best out of 100 other players! It's also more child-friendly than most other shooter games.

Valorant

This is a more competitive game that challenges only the best shooter game enthusiasts. (would NOT recommend if you get made easily)

Mario Kart 8 Deluxe

This is a classic driving game that is fun for people of all ages to play. Regardless of skill level, it's very fun! Like valorant, I DO NOT recommend for people who get mad easily...
